Simple Baileys Ice Cream

A sweet dessert for those with a child at heart.

Keep it away from real kids.

Ingredients:

  • Baileys or any cream liqueur
  • Milk Ice Cream or any almost plain flavoured

Steps:

  1. Mix ice cream with the cream liqueur in a 1:1 ratio
  2. The ice cream must melt into a slushie to combine with the liqueur
  3. Let the mixture freeze for a couple hours
  4. Serve it
You can skip the last steps and enjoy a sweet and yet alcoholic slushie!
